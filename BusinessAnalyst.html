Part-Time Business Analyst

Location: Remote (U.S.-based preferred)

Company: Omnera LLC

Hours: 15–25 hours per week

Compensation: Competitive hourly rate (based on experience)

Position Overview

Omnera LLC is seeking a skilled Part-Time Business Analyst to support systems integration, business process optimization, and enterprise platform enhancements. This role is ideal for a professional who can bridge business strategy with technical execution by translating organizational needs into structured system requirements and ensuring successful implementation across digital platforms.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze Omnera’s existing information systems architecture and evaluate business and technical requirements for integration with third-party services and enterprise platforms.
  • Consult with business stakeholders and end users to analyze and define functional, technical, and operational system requirements.
  • Prepare and maintain formal Business Requirements Documents (BRDs), system specifications, workflow diagrams, and process documentation to translate business needs into technical solutions.
  • Serve as a liaison between business stakeholders and technical development teams to ensure system design and implementation align with documented requirements and organizational objectives.
  • Coordinate and document system testing activities, including user acceptance validation (UAT), to confirm that implemented solutions meet defined performance and functional standards.
  • Assess system performance and user feedback to recommend enhancements, process improvements, and system optimization strategies to improve efficiency and integration stability.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
  • 10+ years of enterprise systems analysis experience
  • Demonstrated expertise in analyzing, designing, and validating complex system workflows
  • Experience with vendor integrations, logistics integrations, and large-scale system deployments
  • Proven experience developing and managing BRDs from scope definition through stakeholder sign-off
  • Strong experience coordinating UAT, SIT, regression testing, and cross-functional validation efforts
  • Experience working closely with DevOps and technical teams to translate business requirements into implementable solutions
  • Strong documentation, analytical, and communication skills

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with cloud-native systems and enterprise platforms
  • Familiarity with enterprise application integrations and third-party service architecture
  • Background supporting digital platforms, CRM, ERP, or operational systems
  • Experience in consulting, systems transformation, or multi-department environments

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Strong stakeholder management and facilitation skills
  • Capable of independently managing end-to-end requirements lifecycle
  • Experienced in cross-functional collaboration across business, development, and operations teams
