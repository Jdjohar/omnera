Part-Time Business Analyst

Location: Remote (U.S.-based preferred)
Hours: 15–25 hours per week
Compensation: Competitive hourly rate (based on experience)

Position Overview

Omnera LLC is seeking a skilled Part-Time Business Analyst to support systems integration, business process optimization, and enterprise platform enhancements. This role bridges business strategy with technical execution by translating organizational needs into structured system requirements and ensuring successful implementation across digital platforms.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze existing information systems architecture and evaluate integration requirements.
  • Consult with stakeholders to define functional, technical, and operational requirements.
  • Develop and maintain Business Requirements Documents (BRDs), system specifications, and workflow documentation.
  • Act as liaison between business stakeholders and technical teams.
  • Coordinate system testing including UAT, SIT, and regression testing.
  • Assess system performance and recommend process improvements and optimization strategies.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
  • 10+ years of enterprise systems analysis experience.
  • Expertise in analyzing and validating complex system workflows.
  • Experience with vendor and logistics integrations.
  • Proven experience developing and managing BRDs.
  • Strong experience coordinating UAT, SIT, and cross-functional validation efforts.
  • Experience working with DevOps and technical teams.
  • Strong documentation, analytical, and communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with cloud-native systems and enterprise platforms.
  • Familiarity with enterprise application integrations and third-party architecture.
  • Background supporting CRM, ERP, or operational systems.
  • Experience in consulting or systems transformation environments.

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Structured, process-driven, and detail-oriented.
  • Strong stakeholder management and facilitation skills.
  • Capable of independently managing the full requirements lifecycle.
  • Experienced in cross-functional collaboration.

Why Join Us?

  • Flexible part-time schedule.
  • Direct collaboration with leadership.
  • Exposure to technology consulting and digital commerce operations.
  • Opportunity to contribute to scalable system architecture.

To Apply

Please submit your resume along with a brief description of your experience with system architecture analysis, requirements documentation, and integration projects.
