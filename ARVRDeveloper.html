AR/VR Developer

Location: Lisle, IL | Job Type: Full-Time

About Us

Omnera LLC is a leading innovator in furniture design and retail, blending craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. We are looking for an AR/VR Developer to enhance our customers' virtual shopping experience by focusing on the integration and optimization of AR features for virtual room and furniture design. This role emphasizes evaluating existing tools and technologies to improve or build upon them when necessary.

Key Responsibilities

  • Tool Evaluation and Optimization: Assess and analyze existing AR/VR tools for their suitability in virtual furniture design and integration with our product catalog.
  • Feature Development: Enhance and customize AR tools to support virtual furniture placement, room visualization, and interactive design features.
  • Gap Identification: Identify gaps in current tools and propose new features or entirely new tools if necessary.
  • Integration: Work closely with designers, developers, and product teams to seamlessly integrate AR features into our customer-facing platforms.
  • 3D Asset Handling: Collaborate with the design team to incorporate 3D models of furniture into AR environments, ensuring high-quality visualization and accurate scaling.
  • User Interaction Design: Develop and refine intuitive, user-friendly AR interfaces to allow customers to visualize and customize furniture layouts within their spaces.
  • Performance Tuning: Optimize AR/VR experiences for various devices, ensuring smooth performance and a low-latency experience.
  • Cross-Platform Support: Adapt AR/VR solutions for deployment across multiple platforms, including iOS, Android, and AR/VR headsets like HoloLens or Magic Leap.
  • Technology Monitoring: Stay updated with the latest developments in AR/VR technologies and evaluate their potential integration into our tools.
