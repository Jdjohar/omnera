UI/UX Designer

Location: Lisle, IL | Job Type: Full-Time

Apply Now

About Us

Omnera LLC is at the forefront of innovation in the furniture and home furnishing industry. We combine exceptional design with cutting-edge technology to create memorable customer experiences. We are seeking a talented UI/UX Designer to craft intuitive, visually compelling interfaces for our virtual room staging platform powered by AR technology.

As part of our team, you will help customers visualize their dream spaces by enabling furniture selection from multiple catalogs, providing intelligent design suggestions, and offering options to rent or purchase furniture—all through a seamless and engaging user experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design AR Experiences: Create user-friendly interfaces for AR-based virtual room staging that allow customers to visualize furniture placement in real-time.
  • Multi-Catalog Integration: Design intuitive navigation systems for browsing and selecting furniture from multiple product catalogs, with features for intelligent recommendations based on user preferences.
  • Customization and Recommendations: Develop features for personalized design recommendations, enabling users to customize furniture styles, layouts, and colors.
  • Seamless Rental/Purchase Options: Design user flows for renting or purchasing furniture, ensuring a smooth and secure transaction process.
  • Prototyping and Wireframing: Create wireframes, prototypes, and user journey maps to communicate design concepts and iterate based on feedback.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with product managers, AR/VR developers, and marketing teams to align the user experience with business goals and technical feasibility.
  • User Research: Conduct user interviews, usability tests, and gather feedback to identify pain points and refine the user experience.
  • Brand Consistency: Ensure all designs align with the company’s brand identity and resonate with the target audience.
  • Performance Monitoring: Collaborate with data analysts to measure the success of UI/UX designs and suggest iterative improvements.

Qualifications

Required:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in UI/UX design, with a portfolio showcasing relevant projects.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or similar software.
  • Experience designing AR or VR interfaces, particularly for e-commerce or retail applications.
  • Strong understanding of interaction design principles and responsive design.
  • Familiarity with user research methods and usability testing.
  • Ability to design for cross-platform experiences (web, mobile, AR).
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Preferred:

  • Experience in the furniture or home furnishing industry.
  • Knowledge of AR platforms (e.g., ARKit, ARCore) and their design implications.
  • Understanding of personalization algorithms and intelligent recommendation systems.
  • Background in e-commerce design, including cart, checkout, and payment workflows.

What We Offer

  • A creative, supportive, and innovative work environment.
  • Opportunities for professional growth and learning.
  • Flexible work arrangements, including remote options.
